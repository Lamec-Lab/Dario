\subsection{Objectives}

% \subsection{General Objective}
The primary objective of this research is to analyze vibration mitigation on tendon-like structures for TLPs. To this end, the study employs periodic Euler-Bernoulli beams, configured as both stepped and bi-material structures, as simplified representations to investigate the effectiveness of periodicity as a passive strategy for reducing mechanical vibrations. The following objectives work as a basis towards that goal:

\begin{itemize}
    \item[\blacktriangleright] Develop an analytical model for the dynamic response of periodic beams based on the Stepped Euler-Bernoulli Beam Theory (EBT).
    \item[\blacktriangleright] Implement a numerical model using the Finite Element Method (FEM) in a MATLAB\textsuperscript{\textregistered} environment to solve the generalized eigenvalue problem associated with the proposed configurations.
    \item[\blacktriangleright] Analyze the formation and location of frequency band gaps in both stepped (geometric periodicity) and bimaterial (material periodicity) beam configurations.
    \item[\blacktriangleright] Evaluate the influence of unit cell configuration on the natural frequencies, and resulting band gaps generated for the structure.
\end{itemize}
